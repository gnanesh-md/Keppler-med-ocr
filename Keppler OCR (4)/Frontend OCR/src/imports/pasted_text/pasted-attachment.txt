Design a complete enterprise-grade UI/UX prototype for "Keppler AI", an AI-powered Medical Document Intelligence Platform used by hospitals, doctors, pharmacists, medical coders, and administrators.

This is NOT a dashboard application.

The experience should feel like a professional SaaS workspace similar to Notion, Linear, Arc Browser, Microsoft Loop, Figma, and Epic Electronic Health Record (EHR), with task-oriented workspaces instead of analytics dashboards.

The application processes medical documents using OCR, AI summarization, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), document intelligence, and clinical AI.

Design the complete product using a modern medical aesthetic.
